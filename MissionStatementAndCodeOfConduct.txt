Mission Statement:
Our mission with this project is to effectively contribute to an open software program and make noticeable 
improvements to the software. Our goal is to get a better grasp at problem solving and learning the inner 
workings of a project while working as an effective and communicative team.


Code of Conduct:
- Contributors of the open source project shall act consistently with the public interest. 
- Contributors shall consistently act in a manner that is in the best interest for all teammates and project goals.
- Contributors shall ensure that modifications to the project meet a high professional standard.
- Contributors shall consistently communicate with teammates and announce any changes or commits to the repository.
- Contributors shall maintain integrity, avoid blatant plagiarism, and be ethical in their work towards the project. 
- Contributors shall support teammates and work appropriately.
